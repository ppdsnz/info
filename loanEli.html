PPDSNZ LOAN ELIGIBILITY

LOAN

Books can be renewed only once provided they have not been reserved by another patron. Renewals can be done online not later than their due date. Otherwise system is unable to generate request which will leads to fine charges if patron fails to return the book on its due date.

RESERVATIONS


Library items can also be reserved online. Please refer to the Librarian on duty at the Customers Advisory Desk or the circulation counter for further information.
